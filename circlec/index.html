About Us

A family business built on trust and results

Circle C Properties is a full-service real estate brokerage and management company specializing in residential sales, residential management, and HOA management across the DFW metroplex.

Founded in 1991 under the leadership of President Beverly Christensen, our mission is to maintain or grow the value of our clients' assets while providing quality housing for our tenants — through accurate pricing, effective marketing, timely maintenance, and responsive communication.

Selling Your Home

Our 5-step pricing process

Pricing for optimum market placement so your home sells for the highest possible price in the shortest amount of time.

01

Absorption Rate

Homes for sale weighed against buyers in your specific market area.

02
